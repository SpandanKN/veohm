    Welcome to Veohm - Cedar Woods

    Veohm - Cedar Woods, Off Hennur Road | 2BHK 1302 sq.ft. 72L | 3BHK 1529 sq.ft. 83L

    Veohm Cedarwoods is located off Hennur road at K Narayanpura opposite to Kristu Jayanti College. The apartment is well crafted with ground and four upper floors, built on 1.5 acres of land area and is the project of Veohm group. We aim to achieve excellence by rendering quality service to our customers which would help us in building a long term relationship.

    Our project has premium, well designed and spacious flats, with the ground floor apartments having the luxury of private gardens giving the feel of a villa. Large terrace and open space have been provided for social gatherings.
